    Mission

    Establish an innovation and creativity environment to leverage scientific expertise and to contribute to Morocco's energy transition.

    Collaborative Research and Innovation

    • Promote collaborative research projects.
    • Address pressing energy challenges through collaborative efforts.
    • Develop cutting-edge technologies in materials science, energy generation, and technologies.
    • Secure external financing to ensure long-term sustainability.

    Knowledge Exchange and Impact

    • Facilitate cross-disciplinary collaboration among researchers, scientists, and industry experts.
    • Promote the energy transition by disseminating knowledge and best practices.
    • Build capacity through educational initiatives, workshops, seminars, and partnerships.

    Education and Empowerment

    • Provide a platform for students and early-career researchers to engage in impactful research.
    • Offer mentorship and guidance.
    • Create opportunities for skill development and learning within the fields of materials science and energy.

    Overview of MNETC

    The Moroccan National Energy Transition Consortium MNETC. brings together researchers, scholars, industry professionals, and experts from various institutions to exchange and share experiences & knowledge, aiming to push the boundaries of energy science & exploration.
